Anti-Corrosion VCI Packaging

We offer the VCI2000® line of anti-corrosion packaging with VCI (Volatile Corrosion Inhibitor) technology. When metal objects are packed in our VCI products, the VCI molecules volatilize and migrate with the air, then condense on all metal surfaces, reaching exposed and recessed areas.

VCI2000® is your preferred choice for packaging untreated steel parts, corrosion-sensitive metal parts, precision bearings, machined raw steel components or fully assembled deliverables like heavy machinery and military ordinances, eliminating the need for oils, rust removal, industrial degreasers, cleaning chemicals or grease.

Our VCI technology is also 100% nitrite-free, so you get protect your company's products without compromising the environment.

Aerospace & Electronics.

Precision metal.
