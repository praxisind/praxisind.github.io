Lubricants and Resins

We have partnered with US manufacturers of oil, resins and petroleum dyes for use in the oil and gas, energy generation and fleet maintenance verticals. We specialize in countertype substitute products (generics) that perform as well as the leading brands, at a fraction of the cost. Our products are Made In USA. Samples and lab tests provided upon request.

Crankcase lubricants for diesel engines.

Inhibited electrical insulating oils for transformers and electric equipment.

Oxidation-inhibiting oils for bearings and industrial gear systems.

Turbine oils for energy generation and industrial systems.

Greases for automotive, railway and open gear applications.

Anion and cation exchange resins for water demineralization.

Solvent-soluble liquid dyes for fuels and lubricants.
